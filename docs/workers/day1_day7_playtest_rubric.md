# Worker Brief: Day 1-Day 7 Playtest Rubric

## Scope

Run one 20-30 minute clean-save manual playtest of Day 1-Day 7. Do not change code, data, assets, or balance during the run. Use `docs/playtest/day1_day7_playtest_rubric.md` as the source rubric.

## Primary Questions

1. Day 1: Does the player know who 陆小闲 is?
2. Day 2: Does the subway create city pressure?
3. Day 3: Is the convenience store funny but not oily?
4. Skills page: Does it clearly tell the player where to learn skills?
5. Day 5 验货: Does the player feel progress even without a KO?

## Timing

- Day 1 identity: 4 minutes.
- Day 2 subway pressure: 4 minutes.
- Day 3 convenience store comedy: 5 minutes.
- Skills page guidance: 4 minutes.
- Day 4-Day 5 验货 and non-KO progress: 7 minutes.
- Day 6-Day 7 continuity and scoring: 4-6 minutes.

## Reporting Format

Return a short report with:

- Overall verdict: pass, targeted fixes, or needs another pass.
- The eight scores.
- Answers to the five primary questions.
- Top three fixes.
- Any blocker that prevented reaching Day 7.

## Acceptance Gate

The week is ready for broader QA only if every score is 4+ and all five primary questions are answerable without source-code knowledge.
